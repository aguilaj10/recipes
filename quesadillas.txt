Ingredients:
  - Oaxaca cheese
  - Tortillas
  - Avocado
  - Pork sausage

Directions:
1. Fry the pork sausage in a skillet
2. Heat the tortillas in a pan
3. Grate the cheese and put it into the tortillas, add the pork sausage
4. Heat the quesadilla in a pan, turn it constantly till the chess is melted
